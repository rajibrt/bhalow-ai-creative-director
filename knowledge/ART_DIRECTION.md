# Art Direction

## Purpose

This file defines the art direction system for Bhalow. It helps the GPT select backgrounds, lighting, materials, camera angles, moods, props, and composition choices for perfume and beauty e-commerce visuals.

## Creative Direction Summary

Bhalow should look like a premium US fragrance retailer with luxury editorial beauty direction.

The visual world should be:

- Refined
- Minimal
- Product-first
- Commercial-grade
- Elegant
- Trustworthy
- Modern
- Luxurious without clutter

## Core Art Direction Principles

### 1. Product First

The fragrance bottle or product packaging is always the hero. Backgrounds, props, lighting, and typography must support the product, not compete with it.

### 2. Luxury Through Restraint

Luxury does not mean adding more elements. Use fewer, better elements with excellent spacing, premium lighting, and physically believable materials.

### 3. Editorial Composition

Designs should feel like beauty magazine advertising or premium retail merchandising, not marketplace banners.

### 4. Realistic Materials

Glass, crystal, marble, metal, silk, perfume mist, water, and petals should look physically believable.

### 5. Mobile-Safe Design

For Facebook covers and social media assets, important text, logo, and product elements must stay within safe zones.

## Fragrance Family Art Direction

### Floral

Use:

- Soft ivory, blush, champagne
- Petals, jasmine, rose, tuberose, peony
- Morning light
- Soft mist
- Marble or silk

Avoid:

- Overly romantic clutter
- Too many flowers covering the bottle

### Citrus

Use:

- Sunlit ivory, yellow-gold, green accents
- Citrus peel, leaves, water droplets
- Mediterranean garden or clean marble
- Fresh morning light

Avoid:

- Loud yellow backgrounds
- Fruit-market styling

### Woody

Use:

- Warm amber, deep brown, black, gold
- Wood grain, leather, dark stone
- Rim light and warm shadows

Avoid:

- Rustic farmhouse style
- Overly rough textures

### Aquatic / Fresh

Use:

- Crystal water, pale blue, silver, white
- Mist, droplets, glass, smooth stone
- Clean soft light

Avoid:

- Cheap ocean stock-photo style
- Too much blue saturation

### Oriental / Amber

Use:

- Amber glow, gold, smoke, deep shadows
- Polished metal, silk, dark glass
- Warm cinematic light

Avoid:

- Excessive smoke hiding the product
- Overly theatrical backgrounds

### Gourmand

Use:

- Cream, vanilla, caramel, warm gold
- Soft silk, cream marble, edible-inspired hints

Avoid:

- Making the image look like a food ad

## Camera Direction

Preferred camera styles:

- Front three-quarter product angle
- Slight low-angle hero view
- Premium editorial wide shot
- Clean centered product shot for thumbnails
- Wide safe composition for Facebook covers

Avoid:

- Extreme distortion
- Overly dramatic fisheye
- Cropping bottle caps or labels
- Blocking product text with props

## Prop Rules

Props must support the fragrance story.

Good props:

- Petals
- Light mist
- Water droplets
- Crystal pieces
- Marble pedestal
- Silk fabric
- Citrus leaves
- Gold accents
- Soft bokeh

Bad props:

- Too many unrelated objects
- Cheap shopping icons
- Heavy sale stickers
- Confusing decorative clutter

## Final Art Direction Rule

When unsure, choose the cleaner, more premium option. For Bhalow, elegant restraint is better than visual noise.
