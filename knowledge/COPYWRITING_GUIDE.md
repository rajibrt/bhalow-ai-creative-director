# Copywriting Guide

## Purpose

This file defines how Bhalow AI Creative Director should write copy for Bhalow’s US-market fragrance e-commerce visuals, website assets, social media campaigns, and product promotions.

Bhalow copy should feel premium, clear, trustworthy, and modern. It should avoid noisy discount-marketplace language unless the user specifically requests a sale campaign.

## Brand Copy Defaults

- Market: United States
- Language: US English
- Currency: USD
- Website: www.bhalow.com
- Tone: premium, polished, confident, warm, concise
- Style: luxury retail, editorial beauty, product-first

## Copy Personality

Bhalow copy should sound like a refined beauty retailer, not a loud marketplace.

It should be:

- Clear
- Elegant
- Helpful
- Confident
- Honest
- Modern
- Aspirational but not exaggerated

It should not be:

- Loud
- Cheap
- Overhyped
- Spammy
- Pushy
- Full of emojis
- Full of unrealistic claims

## Preferred Words

Use words such as:

- Discover
- Explore
- Curated
- Authentic
- Signature scent
- Designer fragrance
- Luxury fragrance
- Timeless elegance
- Everyday sophistication
- Gift-worthy
- Refined selection
- Premium scent
- Iconic fragrance
- Modern classic
- Fresh arrival
- Beautifully presented

## Words to Avoid Unless Requested

Avoid these unless the user explicitly wants a discount-heavy campaign:

- Cheapest
- Mega sale
- Crazy discount
- Hurry up
- Lowest price ever
- Hot deal
- Wholesale
- Limited stock hurry
- Viral perfume
- Must buy now

## Headline Frameworks

### Signature Scent Framework

Use for general perfume campaigns.

Examples:

- Discover Your Signature Scent
- Find the Fragrance That Feels Like You
- Your Next Signature Fragrance Awaits
- Make Every Moment Unforgettable

### Authenticity Framework

Use when trust is important.

Examples:

- Authentic Designer Fragrances, Curated for You
- Shop Authentic Fragrances with Confidence
- Premium Scents. Trusted Selection.
- Authentic Fragrance, Beautifully Presented

### Gift Framework

Use for seasonal and gifting campaigns.

Examples:

- A Gift That Feels Personal
- Give the Gift of Timeless Elegance
- Fragrances Made for Memorable Moments
- Beautiful Scents for Every Celebration

### Collection Framework

Use for category banners and Facebook covers.

Examples:

- Explore a Curated World of Fragrance
- Premium Fragrances for Every Style
- Designer Scents for Women and Men
- Discover Luxury in Every Bottle

### Fresh Arrival Framework

Use for new product launches.

Examples:

- New Scents, Freshly Curated
- Discover What’s New at Bhalow
- Fresh Arrivals for Your Fragrance Wardrobe
- New Designer Fragrances to Explore

## Subheading Frameworks

Subheadings should provide clarity without becoming long.

Examples:

- Explore authentic designer fragrances for women and men.
- A curated selection of premium scents for every mood, season, and occasion.
- Shop beautifully presented fragrances made for everyday sophistication and memorable gifts.
- Discover iconic perfumes, modern classics, and gift-worthy favorites.

## CTA Library

Use clean, premium CTAs:

- Shop Now
- Explore Collection
- Discover More
- Find Your Signature Scent
- View Collection
- Shop Authentic Fragrances
- Explore New Arrivals
- Shop Gift Sets
- Browse Fragrances

Avoid shouting CTAs like:

- BUY NOW!!!
- ORDER FAST
- GRAB IT NOW
- HURRY UP

## Website Copy Rules

Website copy should prioritize:

1. Product clarity
2. Trust
3. Authenticity
4. Simple navigation
5. Benefit-driven wording

### Product Page Short Description

Structure:

```text
[Product name] is a [fragrance type] designed for [audience/occasion]. Featuring [key notes], it offers a [mood/benefit] scent experience suitable for [use case].
```

Only use notes and claims that are source-backed or provided by the user.

### Category Banner Copy

Structure:

```text
Headline: [Collection benefit]
Subheading: [What the customer can explore]
CTA: [Action]
```

Example:

```text
Headline: Designer Fragrances for Every Occasion
Subheading: Explore authentic scents for everyday wear, gifting, and unforgettable evenings.
CTA: Explore Collection
```

## Social Poster Copy Rules

Social posters need short, readable text.

Recommended structure:

- Headline: 3 to 7 words
- Subheading: 8 to 16 words
- Feature line: notes, size, benefit, or collection cue
- CTA: 1 short phrase
- Website: www.bhalow.com

Avoid long paragraphs on the image.

## Facebook Cover Copy Rules

Facebook covers should be brand-level, not product-page-level.

Recommended copy:

- Headline: broad brand promise
- Subheading: what Bhalow offers
- Feature line: Women • Men • Gift Sets • New Arrivals
- CTA: Shop Now or Explore Collection
- Website: www.bhalow.com

Example:

```text
DISCOVER YOUR SIGNATURE FRAGRANCE
Explore authentic designer perfumes for women and men.
Women • Men • Gift Sets • New Arrivals
SHOP NOW
www.bhalow.com
```

## Email Banner Copy Rules

Email banner copy should be concise and conversion-oriented without being aggressive.

Examples:

- New Designer Fragrances Have Arrived
- Gift-Ready Scents for Every Occasion
- Explore Fresh Picks from Bhalow
- Discover Premium Fragrances for the Season

## SEO Copy Rules

When asked for SEO, use US search behavior.

### SEO Title Formula

```text
[Product Name] | Authentic Designer Fragrance | Bhalow
```

or

```text
Shop [Brand/Product] Perfume Online | Bhalow
```

### Meta Description Formula

```text
Shop [product name] at Bhalow. Discover authentic designer fragrances with premium presentation and a refined online shopping experience.
```

Keep meta descriptions concise and avoid unsupported claims.

## Social Caption Rules

Captions should sound premium and natural.

Structure:

1. Opening line
2. Product or collection benefit
3. Trust or authenticity cue
4. CTA
5. Hashtags, if requested

Example:

```text
Discover a scent that feels unmistakably yours.

Explore authentic designer fragrances curated for everyday elegance, memorable gifts, and signature moments.

Shop now at www.bhalow.com
```

## Hashtag Rules

Use only if requested. Keep hashtags relevant and not excessive.

Examples:

- #Bhalow
- #Fragrance
- #DesignerFragrance
- #LuxuryFragrance
- #PerfumeLovers
- #SignatureScent
- #FragranceCollection

## Claim Safety

Do not claim:

- Best in the world
- Guaranteed compliments
- 100% authentic unless the user confirms this is a permitted brand claim
- Officially authorized unless proven
- Long lasting unless source-backed or provided

Use safer wording:

- Authentic designer fragrances
- Premium selection
- Curated collection
- Crafted for memorable moments
- Designed for everyday sophistication

## Final Copy Rule

Bhalow copy should feel like a premium retail experience. If the copy feels too loud, reduce urgency, remove clutter, and make it more refined.
