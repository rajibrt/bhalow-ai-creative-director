# Brand Foundation

## Brand Identity

**Brand name:** Bhalow  
**Website:** www.bhalow.com  
**Primary market:** United States  
**Category:** Premium fragrance and beauty e-commerce  
**Default language:** US English  
**Default currency:** USD

Bhalow is a US-first premium online fragrance destination focused on authentic designer perfumes, refined product presentation, and a trustworthy shopping experience.

## Brand Positioning

Bhalow should be positioned as a refined online destination for authentic designer fragrances, serving customers across the United States with a premium e-commerce experience.

The brand should feel closer to a curated luxury beauty retailer than a busy marketplace.

## Brand Promise

**Authentic fragrances, elegantly presented, confidently delivered.**

Every Bhalow creative output should reinforce three core promises:

1. Authenticity
2. Premium presentation
3. Customer trust

## Mission

To help US customers discover authentic designer fragrances through a premium digital shopping experience that feels refined, simple, and trustworthy.

## Vision

To become a trusted US online fragrance retailer recognized for authenticity, elegant presentation, and a polished customer experience.

## Brand Personality

Bhalow should always feel:

- Elegant
- Premium
- Modern
- Minimal
- Trustworthy
- Warm
- Refined
- Editorial
- International
- Product-first

Bhalow should never feel:

- Cheap
- Loud
- Flashy
- Overcrowded
- Marketplace-like
- Discount-only
- Confusing
- Low-quality

## Target Audience

### Primary Audience

- Men and women
- Ages 20 to 55
- US-based online shoppers
- Fragrance lovers
- Gift buyers
- Designer fragrance customers
- Beauty and lifestyle shoppers
- Customers who care about authenticity

### Secondary Audience

- Corporate gift buyers
- First-time fragrance shoppers
- Collectors
- Seasonal gift buyers
- Customers shopping for partners, family, or friends

## Brand Voice

Bhalow copy should be:

- Clear
- Premium
- Confident
- Warm
- Informative
- Concise
- Honest
- US-market appropriate

Avoid exaggerated claims and cheap urgency language unless the user explicitly asks for a sale campaign.

## Preferred Vocabulary

Use language such as:

- Discover
- Explore
- Curated
- Authentic
- Signature scent
- Designer fragrance
- Luxury fragrance
- Timeless elegance
- Refined selection
- Premium experience
- Everyday sophistication
- Gift-worthy

Avoid language such as:

- Cheapest
- Mega sale
- Hurry up
- Crazy discount
- Lowest price ever
- Wholesale-style wording
- Overhyped marketplace wording

## Date, Currency, and Measurement Rules

- Use USD by default.
- Use US English spelling: color, favorite, center, personalization.
- Use US date style: July 7, 2026.
- Use fragrance measurements like fl oz and oz by default.
- Use metric only when it is part of the official product information.

## Brand Motto

**Authentic Fragrance. Timeless Elegance. Beautifully Presented.**

## Creative North Star

Every Bhalow design should look like it belongs to a premium US beauty retailer. It should be clean, elegant, product-focused, and trustworthy.
