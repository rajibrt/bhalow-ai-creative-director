# CGI Advertisement Template

## Purpose

Use this template when the user wants a cinematic, premium CGI-style advertisement for a Bhalow fragrance product or fragrance collection.

This template is for high-impact creative scenes, not simple website thumbnails.

## Default Output

- Style: premium CGI commercial advertising
- Market: United States
- Brand: Bhalow
- Category: fragrance and beauty e-commerce
- Product role: product-first hero composition
- Quality target: agency-grade luxury advertising

## Required Inputs

- Product image or product details
- Desired concept, if the user has one

## Optional Inputs

- Logo
- Website
- CTA
- Campaign theme
- Fragrance notes
- Seasonal direction
- Exact aspect ratio

## Auto Decision Rules

Use this template if the user says:

- CGI ad
- Cinematic ad
- Luxury advertisement
- Commercial product ad
- Premium product scene
- Creative campaign visual
- Concept ad

If the user provides no concept, create a concept based on the product analyzer:

- Floral fragrance → botanical luxury scene
- Citrus fragrance → Mediterranean freshness scene
- Woody fragrance → dark luxury wood and amber scene
- Aquatic fragrance → crystal water and glass scene
- Amber/oriental fragrance → warm gold and silk scene
- Men’s fragrance → dark onyx, leather, metal, rim lighting
- Women’s fragrance → marble, silk, floral, champagne glass

## CGI Concept Types

### Product Power Scene

Use when the product should feel bold, iconic, and high value.

### Ingredient World

Use when fragrance notes are known and can guide the scene.

### Luxury Material World

Use when the bottle design suggests glass, metal, marble, leather, silk, or crystal.

### Seasonal Campaign Scene

Use for Christmas, Valentine’s Day, summer, Mother’s Day, Father’s Day, Black Friday, and gifting campaigns.

### Floating Product World

Use for premium surreal visuals, fragrance universe scenes, and social campaigns.

## Prompt Construction Logic

Build the prompt using this structure:

1. Concept name
2. Canvas / ratio
3. Product hero description
4. CGI environment
5. Materials and props
6. Lighting
7. Camera angle
8. Branding, website, and CTA if required
9. Realism and quality constraints
10. Negative constraints

## Final Optimized Prompt Template

```text
Create a premium CGI advertisement for Bhalow, a US-first fragrance e-commerce brand.

Concept:
[concept name]

Canvas:
[aspect ratio or size]. Use a premium commercial advertising composition.

Product:
Use the uploaded fragrance product as the main hero. Keep the bottle and packaging sharp, realistic, fully visible, and accurately proportioned. Preserve the original product identity, bottle shape, cap, label placement, packaging colors, and material feel as much as possible.

CGI Environment:
Build a luxury fragrance world based on the product analysis: [selected environment]. Use refined premium materials such as crystal glass, marble, silk, polished metal, dark stone, water, petals, mist, or warm amber glow only where they support the scent mood.

Composition:
Place the product in a strong hero position with clean negative space for optional branding or campaign copy. Keep the composition cinematic but not cluttered. Ensure the product remains the clearest and most important object.

Lighting:
Use premium commercial beauty lighting with large softbox highlights, realistic rim light, soft fill, cinematic reflections, physically believable shadows, and refined glass caustics.

Camera:
Use a premium advertising camera angle such as front three-quarter view, slight low-angle hero view, or elegant eye-level product shot. Avoid distortion.

Branding:
If the official Bhalow logo is uploaded, use it exactly as provided. Do not redesign, recolor, crop, stretch, rotate, simplify, or recreate it. Include www.bhalow.com and CTA only when requested.

Quality:
Ultra-realistic CGI, agency-grade luxury fragrance advertising, physically accurate materials, sharp product detail, realistic glass, premium reflections, elegant atmosphere, high-end US beauty retail aesthetic.

Important Rules:
Do not cover the product label. Do not crop the bottle. Do not overload the scene with effects. Do not make the background more important than the product. Avoid cheap sale graphics unless specifically requested.
```

## Quality Checklist

- Product remains the hero.
- CGI concept supports the fragrance mood.
- Product label is visible.
- Materials look physically believable.
- Lighting feels premium.
- Scene is cinematic but not cluttered.
- Branding is safe if included.
- Output feels suitable for US luxury fragrance advertising.

## Common Mistakes

Avoid:

- Overly chaotic particles
- Product hidden by smoke or petals
- Unrealistic bottle distortion
- Excessive neon lighting
- Fantasy scene that ignores the product
- Too many unrelated props
- Heavy sale graphics in luxury scenes

## Usage Example

User: Create a luxury CGI ad for this perfume.

Assistant should analyze the product, choose a CGI concept, write a concept name, and generate a cinematic product-first prompt using this template.
