# Social Poster Template

## Purpose

Use this template for square social media posters for Bhalow product promotions, fragrance launches, new arrivals, gift campaigns, and premium fragrance storytelling.

## Default Output

- Ratio: 1:1
- Platform: Instagram feed, Facebook post, social ads
- Style: luxury editorial beauty advertising
- Language: US English
- Brand: Bhalow
- Website: www.bhalow.com

## Required Inputs

- Product image or product details
- Design goal or campaign type

## Optional Inputs

- Bhalow logo
- Price
- Offer
- CTA
- Amazon/product URL
- Product notes
- Campaign theme

## Auto Decision Rules

If the user says any of the following, use this template:

- Social poster
- Instagram poster
- Facebook post
- Product poster
- Square ad
- Luxury poster

If the user provides product notes or a product link, use them to write accurate copy. If no notes are available, write broader premium copy without inventing fragrance details.

## Recommended Copy Structure

- Headline: 3 to 7 words
- Subheading: 8 to 16 words
- Feature line: notes, audience, or product type
- CTA: short and premium
- Website: www.bhalow.com

## Prompt Construction Logic

Build the prompt using this structure:

1. Canvas and brand context
2. Product hero placement
3. Background based on product analyzer
4. Marketing copy placement
5. Logo and website placement
6. CTA style
7. Lighting and material details
8. Quality constraints

## Final Optimized Prompt Template

```text
Create a premium square social media poster for Bhalow, a US-first fragrance e-commerce brand.

Canvas:
1:1 square composition for Instagram and Facebook feed.

Product:
Use the uploaded fragrance product as the main hero. Keep the bottle and packaging sharp, realistic, fully visible, and premium. Preserve the original product identity, shape, label, cap, and color palette as much as possible.

Art Direction:
Create a luxury editorial fragrance campaign using [recommended background based on product analysis]. Use premium materials such as marble, crystal glass, silk, brushed metal, water droplets, soft petals, or refined mist only where they support the fragrance mood.

Composition:
Place the product slightly off-center with generous negative space for premium typography. Keep the layout clean and uncluttered. Do not let props cover the product name or label.

Marketing Copy:
Headline: [headline]
Subheading: [subheading]
Feature Line: [feature line]
CTA: [CTA]
Website: www.bhalow.com

Logo:
If the official Bhalow logo is uploaded, place it cleanly with proper breathing space. Do not redesign, recolor, crop, stretch, or recreate the logo.

Lighting:
Premium commercial beauty lighting with softbox highlights, gentle fill light, subtle rim light, realistic glass reflections, and soft shadows.

Quality:
Ultra-realistic, agency-grade luxury fragrance advertising, sharp product focus, readable typography, premium US-market retail aesthetic, elegant spacing, physically believable materials.

Important Rules:
Do not overcrowd the design. Do not crop the product. Do not distort the product label. Do not use cheap sale graphics unless specifically requested. Keep the design premium, refined, and product-first.
```

## Quality Checklist

- Product is the hero.
- Copy is short and readable.
- Typography does not overlap the product.
- Logo is safe and unchanged.
- Website is correct.
- CTA is clear.
- Background supports the product.
- Design feels premium, not marketplace-like.

## Common Mistakes

Avoid:

- Too many props
- Long copy blocks
- Overly bright colors
- Cheap discount badges
- Logo distortion
- Product label distortion
- Text too close to edges

## Usage Example

User: Create a social poster for this Versace perfume.

Assistant should analyze the product, choose fragrance-matched art direction, write premium copy, and generate a final prompt using this template.
