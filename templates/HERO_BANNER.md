# Hero Banner Template

## Purpose

Use this template for Bhalow website hero banners, collection banners, category banners, landing page headers, and wide promotional banners.

## Default Output

- Format: wide website banner
- Suggested ratio: 21:9, 16:9, or custom website hero size
- Market: United States
- Brand: Bhalow
- Website: www.bhalow.com
- Style: premium fragrance e-commerce, luxury editorial, clean UI-ready layout

## Required Inputs

- Banner purpose
- Product image or category direction

## Optional Inputs

- Logo
- CTA
- Product notes
- Price or offer
- Seasonal theme
- Background reference
- Exact website size

## Auto Decision Rules

Use this template if the user says:

- Hero banner
- Website banner
- Homepage banner
- Category banner
- Collection banner
- Landing page banner
- Header banner

If no product is provided, create a brand/category-level luxury fragrance composition.

If one product is provided, create a single-product hero banner.

If multiple products are provided, create a curated collection hero banner.

## Hero Banner Types

### Homepage Hero

Use for broad Bhalow brand positioning.

Focus:

- Signature fragrance discovery
- Authentic designer perfumes
- Premium shopping experience

### Product Hero

Use when a specific product is promoted.

Focus:

- Product as the visual hero
- Fragrance mood
- Short benefit-led headline

### Collection Hero

Use for women, men, gift sets, new arrivals, or best sellers.

Focus:

- Product variety
- Category clarity
- Premium merchandising

### Seasonal Hero

Use for holidays and campaigns.

Focus:

- Seasonal atmosphere
- Gift appeal
- Clean promotional CTA

## Layout Rules

Website hero banners need UI-safe spacing.

Recommended layout:

- Left or center-left: headline, subheading, CTA
- Right or center-right: product hero
- Top-right or header-safe area: logo if required
- Keep enough space for website UI overlays
- Avoid placing critical text on busy backgrounds

```text
Discover Your Signature Fragrance
```

Homepage subheading:

```text
Explore authentic designer perfumes curated for everyday elegance, gifting, and memorable moments.
```

CTA:

```text
Explore Collection
```

Product hero headline examples:

```text
A Scent Made to Be Remembered
Fresh Elegance, Beautifully Bottled
Luxury Fragrance for Everyday Sophistication
```

## Prompt Construction Logic

Build the prompt using this structure:

1. Banner type and canvas
2. Product or collection role
3. Copy zone and CTA placement
4. Background and art direction
5. Lighting and materials
6. Logo and website rules when needed
7. UI-safe spacing
8. Quality constraints

## Final Optimized Prompt Template

```text
Create a premium website hero banner for Bhalow, a US-first fragrance e-commerce brand.

Canvas:
Wide website hero banner composition, suitable for a luxury e-commerce homepage or landing page. Use a clean 21:9 or 16:9 layout unless a specific size is provided.

Brand:
Bhalow offers authentic designer fragrances with a refined premium shopping experience for the US market.

Product / Collection:
Use the uploaded product or collection as the main visual hero. Keep the product sharp, realistic, fully visible, and premium. Preserve the original bottle shape, cap, label, packaging, and color palette as much as possible.

Layout:
Create a clean landing-page-ready composition with generous negative space for headline, subheading, and CTA. Place the product hero on the opposite side or center-right, depending on the product shape. Keep all important text away from busy areas.

Marketing Copy:
Headline: [headline]
Subheading: [subheading]
CTA: [CTA]
Website: www.bhalow.com, if needed

Logo:
If the official Bhalow logo is uploaded, place it with proper breathing space and do not redesign, recolor, crop, stretch, or recreate it.

Art Direction:
Use a refined luxury fragrance retail environment based on the product analysis. Choose from marble studio, champagne glass gallery, botanical floral scene, Mediterranean citrus light, dark onyx studio, or luxury boutique interior as appropriate.

Lighting:
Premium beauty lighting with large softbox highlights, soft fill light, subtle rim light, realistic shadows, glass reflections, and physically believable product materials.

Quality:
Ultra-realistic, premium US e-commerce hero banner, luxury editorial composition, clean typography zone, agency-grade commercial advertising quality, sharp product detail, elegant spacing.

Important Rules:
Do not overcrowd the banner. Do not crop the product. Do not place copy over the product label. Do not distort the logo. Keep the design clean, premium, UI-safe, and conversion-ready.
```

## Quality Checklist

- Banner has enough negative space for UI and copy.
- Product is strong and not cropped.
- CTA is visible.
- Background supports the product.
- Text does not overlap product label.
- Logo is safe if included.
- Design feels premium and website-ready.

## Common Mistakes

Avoid:

- Overcrowded hero compositions
- Too many products in one banner
- Text over busy backgrounds
- Weak CTA visibility
- Cropped product bottle
- Marketplace-style graphics
- Heavy discount language unless requested

User: Create a website hero banner for this perfume.
