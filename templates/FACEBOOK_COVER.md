# Facebook Cover Template

## Purpose

Use this template when the user wants a premium Facebook cover image for Bhalow.

This template supports:

- Brand-level Facebook covers
- Product collection covers
- Seasonal fragrance covers
- New arrival covers
- Luxury e-commerce campaign covers

## Default Output

- Canvas: Facebook cover composition, 1640 × 624 px
- Market: United States
- Brand: Bhalow
- Website: www.bhalow.com
- Style: luxury fragrance e-commerce, editorial, clean, premium
- Layout: mobile-safe and wide-format aware

## Required Inputs

- Cover goal or campaign type
- Bhalow logo, if the final design must include the actual logo

## Optional Inputs

- Product images
- Product category
- Seasonal theme
- Offer or campaign message
- CTA preference
- Background reference

## Auto Decision Rules

Use this template if the user says:

- Facebook cover
- FB cover
- Cover photo
- Brand cover
- Page cover
- Facebook banner

If the user only says “Create Facebook cover,” assume a brand-level cover for Bhalow.

If product images are provided, create a product collection or product-focused cover.

If no product image is provided, create a brand-level luxury fragrance universe or curated fragrance gallery.

## Cover Types

### Brand Cover

Use when no specific product is provided.

Focus:

- Bhalow brand positioning
- Authentic designer fragrances
- Premium shopping experience
- Broad women and men fragrance collection

### Product Collection Cover

Use when multiple products or a product list is provided.

Focus:

- Fragrance variety
- Curated assortment
- Men’s and women’s categories
- Clean luxury retail composition

### Seasonal Cover

Use when the user mentions holiday, Black Friday, Christmas, Valentine’s Day, Mother’s Day, Father’s Day, summer, or gifting.

Focus:

- Seasonal art direction
- Gift-worthy fragrance positioning
- Premium retail tone

## Layout Rules

Facebook covers require careful spacing.

Recommended layout:

- Left 30%: clean negative space for headline and supporting copy
- Center: hero fragrance composition
- Right 18%: Bhalow logo, website, and CTA
- Keep all important content inside the mobile-safe center area

Avoid putting important text or logo too close to the edges.

## Prompt Construction Logic

Build the prompt using this structure:

1. Brand and canvas
2. Logo rules
3. Website and CTA
4. Background
5. Hero composition
6. Copy placement
7. Lighting
8. Quality rules
9. Facebook-safe constraints

## Final Optimized Prompt Template

```text
Design a premium luxury Facebook cover for Bhalow, a US-first fragrance e-commerce brand.

Canvas:
Facebook cover composition, 1640 × 624 px. Keep all important content inside the mobile-safe center area.

Brand:
Bhalow is a premium online destination for authentic designer fragrances in the United States.

Logo:
Use the uploaded official Bhalow logo exactly as provided. Do not redesign, recolor, crop, stretch, rotate, simplify, or recreate the logo. Preserve its original proportions and transparency. Place the logo in the upper-right safe area with generous breathing space.

Website:
Display www.bhalow.com below the logo.

CTA:
Add a clean luxury CTA button: SHOP NOW.

Creative Direction:
Luxury beauty campaign, premium fragrance retail, editorial minimalism, clean US-market e-commerce branding, refined commercial advertising quality.

Background:
Soft ivory to champagne gradient with subtle marble texture, elegant glass reflections, soft perfume mist, refined bokeh, warm premium lighting, and subtle golden particles.

Composition:
Leave approximately 30% clean negative space on the left for headline and supporting copy. Leave approximately 18% clean space on the right for logo, website, and CTA. Keep the layout balanced, spacious, and premium.

Hero Composition:
Create a curated luxury fragrance gallery in the center with elegant perfume bottles for women and men. Use varied premium bottle shapes, crystal glass, metallic caps, beautiful colored fragrance liquids, realistic reflections, and refined product photography aesthetics. The collection should feel diverse but not crowded.

Typography:
Headline: DISCOVER YOUR SIGNATURE FRAGRANCE
Subheading: Explore authentic designer perfumes for women and men.
Feature Line: Women • Men • Gift Sets • New Arrivals

Lighting:
Premium commercial beauty lighting with large softbox highlights, soft fill light, subtle rim light, realistic glass reflections, gentle shadows, and refined luxury glow.

Quality:
Ultra-realistic, agency-grade luxury fragrance advertising, clean composition, premium US beauty retail aesthetic, physically believable materials, sharp details, readable typography.

Important Rules:
Do not crop important products. Do not distort the logo. Do not overcrowd the banner. Do not place important content near the edges. Keep the cover elegant, mobile-safe, and brand-level.
```

## Quality Checklist

- Mobile-safe layout is maintained.
- Logo is in the upper-right safe area.
- Website is correct.
- CTA is readable.
- Left side has clean copy space.
- Product gallery is premium and not crowded.
- Typography is readable.
- Overall design feels like a US luxury fragrance retailer.

## Common Mistakes

Avoid:

- Too much text
- Logo too close to edge
- Busy product collage
- Cropped bottles
- Heavy sale graphics
- Marketplace-style badges
- Low-contrast typography
