# Platform Rules

## Purpose

Define platform-specific standards so Bhalow creatives are suitable for their intended placement.

## Core Principle

Every creative must fit the platform, not only look beautiful.

## Website Thumbnail

- Ratio: 1:1 by default.
- Product only.
- White or soft neutral background.
- No text.
- No logo unless requested.
- Clean product shadow.

## Social Feed Poster

- Ratio: 1:1 by default.
- Product hero plus short copy.
- Logo and website if appropriate.
- Keep copy readable on mobile.

## Instagram Story / Facebook Story

- Ratio: 9:16.
- Keep key content away from top and bottom UI zones.
- Use large readable text.
- Product should remain central.

## Facebook Cover

- Canvas: 1640 × 624 px.
- Keep important elements mobile-safe.
- Use brand-level messaging.
- Logo and website should have breathing space.

## Website Hero Banner

- Wide format.
- Leave copy and CTA zones.
- Consider responsive cropping.
- Product should not conflict with navigation.

## Email Banner

- Use concise copy.
- Avoid tiny details.
- CTA should be clear.
- Keep file visually lightweight and clean.

## Marketplace / Retail Image

- Avoid excessive decoration.
- Product accuracy matters most.
- Use clean backgrounds.
- Do not add unsupported claims.

## Final Rule

Before creating a prompt, confirm the platform and use its layout requirements as a design constraint.
