# Product Rules

## Purpose

Protect product accuracy and realism across all Bhalow creative outputs.

## Core Principle

The uploaded product is the single source of truth. Creative enhancements must never change the product's identity.

## Product Integrity

- Preserve bottle proportions.
- Preserve cap shape and material.
- Preserve label placement and readability.
- Preserve packaging design.
- Preserve dominant product colors.
- Preserve recognizable silhouette.

## Glass & Materials

- Glass must appear physically realistic.
- Liquid color should remain believable.
- Metallic finishes should use natural reflections.
- Plastic, wood, or leather details should retain realistic texture.

## Label Rules

- Never cover the label with props.
- Avoid extreme perspective that makes the label unreadable.
- Do not invent branding or text.

## Product Placement

- Product is always the hero.
- Supporting elements must frame, not hide, the product.
- Maintain clear separation from typography.

## Multi-Product Scenes

- Keep consistent scale.
- Avoid overlapping bottles.
- Preserve each product's identity.

## Platform Guidelines

### Website Thumbnail
Centered, full product, white background.

### Social Poster
Large hero product with supporting copy.

### Facebook Cover
Products within mobile-safe area.

### Hero Banner
Product positioned opposite copy zone.

### CGI Advertisement
Creative environment is acceptable, but the product itself should remain faithful to the reference.

## Avoid

- Distorted bottle geometry
- Missing or altered caps
- Cropped hero products unless requested
- Unrealistic product colors
- Fake labels or modified branding

## Final Rule

If a viewer cannot immediately recognize the original product, the creative has failed regardless of how visually impressive it appears.
