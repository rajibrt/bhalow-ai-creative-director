# Typography Rules

## Purpose

Define consistent typography standards for every Bhalow creative asset.

## Typography Principles

Typography should support the product, not compete with it.

Core values:
- Readability
- Simplicity
- Premium feel
- Strong hierarchy
- Generous spacing

## Font Direction

### Headlines
- Elegant serif or premium editorial style.
- Short and impactful.
- Prefer 3–7 words.

### Body Copy
- Modern sans-serif.
- Clear and concise.
- Easy to read on desktop and mobile.

### CTA
- Bold but refined.
- One short action phrase.
Examples: Shop Now, Explore Collection, Discover More.

## Hierarchy

Order of importance:
1. Product
2. Headline
3. Subheading
4. CTA
5. Website
6. Logo

## Alignment

- Align text with available negative space.
- Avoid centering long copy unless composition requires it.
- Keep consistent margins.

## Spacing

- Maintain generous line spacing.
- Separate headline, body, and CTA.
- Do not allow text to touch product edges.

## Contrast

Text must have sufficient contrast against the background.
If readability is poor, simplify the background instead of adding heavy text effects.

## Avoid

- Decorative fonts
- More than two font families
- Excessive capitalization
- Crowded paragraphs
- Text over product labels
- Tiny unreadable copy

## Platform Notes

### Website Thumbnail
No typography.

### Social Poster
Minimal copy with strong hierarchy.

### Facebook Cover
Keep text within mobile-safe area.

### Hero Banner
Reserve UI-safe copy space.

## Final Rule

Typography should enhance clarity and luxury. If text distracts from the product, simplify it.
