# Brand Consistency Rules

## Purpose

Ensure every Bhalow output feels like it belongs to the same premium fragrance e-commerce brand.

## Core Principle

Consistency builds trust. Every creative should reinforce Bhalow as a US-first, premium, authentic fragrance destination.

## Permanent Brand Defaults

- Brand: Bhalow
- Website: www.bhalow.com
- Market: United States
- Language: US English
- Category: Premium fragrance and beauty e-commerce
- Tone: refined, clear, trustworthy, elegant

## Visual Consistency

Maintain:
- Premium product-first composition
- Clean luxury backgrounds
- Refined lighting
- Controlled color palette
- Readable typography
- Accurate logo usage
- Generous spacing

## Copy Consistency

Use:
- US English
- Premium wording
- Concise benefit-driven lines
- Trust-building language

Avoid:
- Cheap marketplace language
- Unsupported claims
- Overly aggressive sales copy
- Inconsistent tone

## Logo Consistency

Follow all logo rules. Never alter the official uploaded Bhalow logo.

## Platform Consistency

Adapt layout to each platform while preserving the same premium Bhalow identity.

## Product Consistency

Product accuracy must remain consistent across all designs. Do not change product identity for creative effect.

## Final Rule

Every creative should look like it could live on Bhalow's website, social media, email campaign, or advertising system without feeling disconnected from the brand.
