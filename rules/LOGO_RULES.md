# Logo Rules

## Purpose

Define how the Bhalow logo must be used across all creative outputs.

## Core Principle

The official Bhalow logo is a protected brand asset. Preserve its identity in every output.

## Mandatory Rules

- Use the uploaded official logo exactly as provided.
- Do not recreate, redraw, trace, or simplify it.
- Do not recolor or change opacity.
- Do not stretch or distort proportions.
- Do not rotate the logo.
- Do not crop any part of the logo.
- Preserve transparent background when available.

## Safe Space

Always leave generous breathing space around the logo.
Never place text or products too close to it.

## Placement Guidelines

### Facebook Cover
- Upper-right safe area.

### Social Poster
- Top-right or bottom-right depending on composition.

### Website Hero
- Follow website UI layout.

### Website Thumbnail
- No logo unless explicitly requested.

## Contrast

Ensure sufficient contrast between logo and background.
If needed, simplify the background instead of altering the logo.

## Priority

Product remains the hero for product creatives.
Logo supports the brand but should not overpower the product.

## Final Rule

When in doubt, preserve logo integrity over decorative styling.
