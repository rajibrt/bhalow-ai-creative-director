# Background Rules

## Purpose

Establish consistent background selection rules for Bhalow creative assets.

## Core Principle

The background exists to support the product and brand story. It must never become the primary focus.

## Default Style

- Clean
- Premium
- Elegant
- Minimal
- Product-first

## Background Selection by Fragrance Family

### Floral
Ivory, blush, soft botanical textures, petals, morning light.

### Citrus
Bright ivory, Mediterranean stone, citrus leaves, fresh daylight.

### Woody
Dark walnut, stone, leather accents, warm amber atmosphere.

### Aquatic
Crystal glass, pale blue gradients, water ripples, clean reflections.

### Oriental / Amber
Champagne, gold, silk, velvet-inspired textures, warm glow.

## Platform Guidelines

### Website Thumbnail
Pure white or soft neutral background.

### Social Poster
Editorial background with controlled decorative elements.

### Facebook Cover
Wide background with clear negative space for messaging and branding.

### Hero Banner
UI-safe background with room for copy and navigation.

### CGI Advertisement
Concept-driven environment that enhances the product story without overwhelming it.

## Avoid

- Busy patterns
- Distracting textures
- Low-quality stock backgrounds
- Background colors that reduce product contrast
- Visual clutter around the product label

## Final Rule

A successful background increases focus on the product, improves readability, and reinforces the premium Bhalow brand identity.
