# Mobile Safe Rules

## Purpose

Define mobile-safe layout standards for Bhalow creatives, especially Facebook covers, hero banners, and social media assets.

## Core Principle

Important content must remain visible, readable, and balanced across desktop and mobile views.

## Facebook Cover Rules

- Keep the most important content inside the central mobile-safe area.
- Do not place logo, CTA, or headline too close to the left or right edges.
- Place the Bhalow logo in the upper-right safe area only if there is enough breathing space.
- Keep product heroes away from extreme edges.
- Avoid placing small text near edges.

## Social Poster Rules

- Keep text away from the outer margins.
- Maintain readable headline size.
- Avoid placing CTA too close to the bottom edge.
- Make sure product and headline are visible at small screen size.

## Hero Banner Rules

- Leave UI-safe negative space for website navigation, buttons, and responsive cropping.
- Do not place essential product details in areas likely to crop on mobile.
- Keep headline and CTA in a clean readable zone.

## Safe Margin Guidance

- Use generous margins.
- Keep important information within the central 70–80% of the design.
- Avoid dense copy near edges.

## Avoid

- Edge-to-edge important text
- Logo touching borders
- CTA placed in crop-prone zones
- Product label near extreme edges
- Tiny text that fails on mobile

## Final Rule

A Bhalow creative should remain clear and premium even when viewed on a small mobile screen.
