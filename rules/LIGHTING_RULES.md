# Lighting Rules

## Purpose

Define premium lighting standards for Bhalow fragrance and beauty creatives.

## Core Principle

Lighting should enhance product realism, luxury perception, and readability without distracting from the product.

## Default Lighting Style

- Large soft key light
- Gentle fill light
- Subtle rim light
- Realistic reflections
- Soft natural shadows

## Lighting by Product Type

### Floral
Soft morning light, airy highlights, delicate glow.

### Citrus
Bright fresh daylight, crisp reflections, energetic feel.

### Woody / Leather
Warm directional lighting, amber highlights, deeper shadows.

### Aquatic
Cool clean light, crystal reflections, water-inspired clarity.

### Oriental / Amber
Warm cinematic lighting with refined golden accents.

## Platform Guidelines

### Website Thumbnail
Even commercial lighting with accurate colors.

### Social Poster
Editorial beauty lighting with product-first emphasis.

### Facebook Cover
Balanced lighting across the full canvas; avoid overly dark edges.

### Hero Banner
Keep copy area evenly lit for readability.

### CGI Advertisement
Use cinematic lighting while maintaining physically believable materials.

## Avoid

- Harsh blown highlights
- Pure black shadow areas hiding product details
- Unrealistic colored lighting unless requested
- Excessive lens flares
- Overly dramatic effects that reduce product visibility

## Final Rule

If lighting makes the product harder to identify or read, simplify it. Premium lighting should make the product look desirable, authentic, and easy to understand.
