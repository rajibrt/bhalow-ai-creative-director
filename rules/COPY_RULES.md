# Copy Rules

## Purpose

Define consistent copy standards for every Bhalow creative asset.

## Core Principle

Copy should support trust, clarity, and conversion without overpowering the visual design.

## Tone

- Premium
- Clear
- Confident
- Helpful
- Concise
- US English by default

## Headline Rules

- Prefer 3–7 words.
- Focus on one idea.
- Lead with benefit or emotion.
- Avoid clickbait.

## Subheading Rules

- One concise supporting sentence.
- Explain value without repeating the headline.

## CTA Rules

Preferred examples:
- Shop Now
- Explore Collection
- Discover More
- Find Your Signature Scent

Avoid:
- BUY NOW!!!
- Hurry!!!
- Lowest Price Ever

## Platform Guidelines

### Website Thumbnail
No copy.

### Social Poster
Headline + short supporting line + CTA.

### Facebook Cover
Brand-level headline with minimal supporting copy.

### Hero Banner
Benefit-led headline, concise subheading, clear CTA.

## Claims

Do not make unsupported claims about authenticity, longevity, awards, or performance.

## Readability

- Keep text brief.
- Use sentence case or tasteful title case.
- Avoid large paragraphs on images.

## Final Rule

Every line of copy should either build trust, explain value, or encourage action. Remove any text that does not serve one of those goals.
