# Color Rules

## Purpose

Define color selection standards for Bhalow creatives to maintain a premium, consistent luxury brand experience.

## Core Principle

Colors should reinforce the product, fragrance mood, and brand identity. They should never overpower the hero product.

## Default Brand Direction

- Elegant neutrals
- Soft ivory
- Champagne
- Warm gold accents
- Deep charcoal
- Rich navy for technology-inspired layouts when appropriate

## Fragrance Family Palettes

### Floral
Ivory, blush, soft pink, cream, muted lavender, fresh green accents.

### Citrus
White, pale yellow, fresh green, light stone, Mediterranean blue accents.

### Woody
Walnut brown, cedar, dark bronze, warm amber, charcoal.

### Aquatic
White, crystal blue, silver, cool gray, transparent glass tones.

### Oriental / Amber
Gold, champagne, caramel, deep burgundy, warm black.

### Gourmand
Cream, vanilla, cocoa, caramel, soft brown.

## Contrast Rules

- Ensure product stands out clearly.
- Maintain readable typography.
- Avoid low-contrast combinations.

## Platform Guidelines

### Website Thumbnail
Neutral colors only.

### Social Poster
Accent colors may support the campaign but must remain balanced.

### Facebook Cover
Use a restrained palette with generous negative space.

### Hero Banner
Keep colors aligned with the landing page experience.

## Avoid

- Oversaturated backgrounds
- Random rainbow palettes
- Neon colors unless specifically requested
- Conflicting color temperatures

## Final Rule

Every color decision should improve clarity, elegance, and product focus while remaining consistent with Bhalow's premium US-market positioning.
